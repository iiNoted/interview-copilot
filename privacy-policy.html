Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Interview Copilot ("the App") is developed by SourceThread. This policy describes how we handle your data.

Data Collection

The App processes the following data locally on your device:

  • Audio transcription: System audio is captured and transcribed in real-time using on-device speech recognition (whisper.cpp). Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Resume & job descriptions: Documents you upload are stored locally on your device and used only to provide contextual AI responses.
  • Transcript history: Transcripts are saved locally and can be exported or deleted at any time.

AI Processing

When you ask the AI assistant a question, your query and relevant context (transcript excerpts, resume/job description text) are sent to:

  • Anthropic API (Claude) — governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • Or OpenClaw (self-hosted) — data stays on your local network

You choose which backend to use in Settings. No AI queries are made without your explicit action.

Billing

If you use the paid tier, usage data (token counts, not content) is reported to Stripe for billing. Stripe handles payment processing under their own privacy policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information, analytics, or telemetry
  • We do not track your usage patterns or behavior
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not store audio recordings

Remote View

The optional Remote View feature streams your overlay state to your phone over your local network. This data is encrypted in transit and never leaves your network unless you explicitly configure an external URL.

Data Storage & Deletion

All data is stored locally in your macOS Application Support directory. Uninstalling the App removes all data. You can also delete individual transcripts, resumes, and job descriptions from within the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
